Worldwide Newton Association

Welcome to the front desk of the Worldwide Newton Association.

The Worldwide Newton Association is a legal structure that was founded in April 2004.

It gathers Newton users around the world. It provides them with regular news about the Newton community and it organizes international events, meetings, symposiums and conferences dedicated to the Newton.

The Worldwide Newton Conference held in 2004 and the upcoming 2006 conference are events presented by thge WWNA.

The Worldwide Newton Association is proud to have John Sculley as its honourary president and to count Larry Yaeger and Walter Smith among its honourary members.

You can join the Worldwide Newton Association and benefit from all the advantages the association provides to its members.

The current Worldwide Newton Association bureau consists in the following community members:

  • Ronnie Simon (UK), President
  • Paul Guyot (FR), Treasurer

Don't hesitate to contact us if you have any question. You can also consult the association's press releases.

Donations are gladly accepted in support of the association.
