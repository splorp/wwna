Worldwide Newton Association

Welcome.

The Worldwide Newton Association is a legal structure, founded in April 2004.

The association gathers Newton users from around the world, providing news about the Newton community, as well as international events, meetings, symposiums, and conferences dedicated to the Newton.

The Worldwide Newton Conference is sponsored and facilitated by the WWNA. The conference has been held in Paris (2004), San Fransisco (2006), and Tokyo (2007).

The Worldwide Newton Association is proud to have John Sculley as its honourary president and to count Larry Yaeger and Walter Smith among its honourary members.

You can join the Worldwide Newton Association and benefit from all the advantages the association provides to its members.

The current Worldwide Newton Association bureau consists in the following community members:

  • Ronnie Simon (UK), President
  • Paul Guyot (FR), Treasurer

Please contact us if you have any questions. You can also consult the association’s press release archive for additional background information.

Donations are gladly accepted in support of the association.
