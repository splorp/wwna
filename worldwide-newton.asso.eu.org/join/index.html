Worldwide Newton Association

The Worldwide Newton Association would be pleased to count you among its members.

Both individuals and companies can be members of the assocation.

In addition to being able to vote and decide the future of the association, members are offered the following three advantages by the association:

  • Members get a frequent newsletter by e-mail or regular mail. This newsletter will summarize the news of the Newton community and members will hence be aware of the important changes and new things available for the Newton. The association also forwards to each member, by e-mail, last minute alerts about Newton User Group meetings in their local area.
  • Members get a reduced fees participation to take part to events organized by the association.
  • Members will also get access to the contact information of other members and hence get the ability to contact them for an improvised Newton User Group meeting.

Additionally, the Worldwide Newton Association can provide its infrastructure to help projects within the Newton community.

Membership fee depends on your status:

Regular membership: 30 euros per year
(Equivalent in CAD | CHF | GBP | JPY | USD)
Student/Unemployed membership: 15 euros per year
(Equivalent in CAD | CHF | GBP | JPY | USD)
Corporate membership: 300 euros per year
(Equivalent in CAD | CHF | GBP | JPY | USD)

Additionally, you can become a donor member. Donor's names and URLs appear on the website. Contact us for more information.

You currently can subscribe via PayPal (no PayPal account is required). The association's PayPal account is paypal@worldwide-newton.asso.eu.org

Important: the Worldwide Newton Association is a legal structure belonging to its members and its detailed balance and account records are presented by the treasurer every year during the annual general meeting and can be sent to any member asking for it.
