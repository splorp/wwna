Worldwide Newton Association

The Worldwide Newton Association is an association governed by French Law.

Download the current rules (in French) and an Unofficial Translation (in English).

According to these rules, the association (like many associations under French Law) is directed by a board (up to 12 members, elected for three years during general annual meetings) and a bureau which is a subset of the board (President, Secretary, Treasurer, Vice-president(s) and Deputy Secretary/Treasurer).

The board was renewed during the inaugural general meeting September 5th, 2004. It includes the following members:

  • Paul Guyot, Treasurer
